Welcome to Mobilize CRM

Mobilize CRM is a powerful tool designed exclusively for our company to manage and nurture our network of contacts, churches, and partners.

With Mobilize CRM, you can:

  • Efficiently manage contacts, including individuals and churches
  • Track communication history and maintain detailed logs
  • Manage tasks and stay on top of your workflow
  • Monitor and advance prospects through your pipeline
  • Generate insightful reports to guide your outreach strategies

How to Get Access

Mobilize CRM is an internal tool for authorized company personnel only. If you're a member of our team and need access, please contact your supervisor or the IT department.

For more information about Mobilize CRM, please email crm-support@mobilizecrm.com.
